Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a carpet clean usually take?

This obviously depends on the size of the room or house etc but one room would take around 90 mins start to finish including the set of up of the airflex carpet cleaning machine. This is one of the most effective professional carpet cleaning machines available on the market.

Can I use my carpets straight after cleaning?

Carpets and rugs can be used immediately after cleaning and will normally be dry after a few hours due to the airflex carpet cleaning machines excellent extraction capabilities (if carpet is heavily soiled drying time may vary).

What areas do you service for carpet cleaning?

We cover most areas in the Devon area for carpet cleaning including: Bideford, Barnstaple, South Molton, Tiverton, Exeter, Ilfracombe and Bude.

Are your carpet cleaning products pet and children friendly?

We use environmentally friendly cleaning products to provide an all natural cleaning solution. Our unique solution not only smells great but is child and pet friendly.

What types of carpets can be cleaned?

All types of carpets can be cleaned, and in most cases can be brought back to a very good condition negating the need to purchase new carpets.
