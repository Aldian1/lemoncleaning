The Oven Cleaning Process

All racks from the oven are placed into a specially built dip tank along with back plates, glass bulb covers, rubber seals and glass from your oven doors depending if it can be removed. We then start on the oven clean its self.

The oven is cleaned using a blade to scrape off all the burnt carbon and then the oven is scrubbed using specialised oven cleaning solutions and products which will make your ovens sparkle again.

Once the racks have had enough time in our tanks they are removed and rinsed then dried before going back in your oven sparkling for a professional finish every time. Even if your oven seems in a very poor condition, it can often be given a new lease of life through our process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to clean my oven?

It depends on the size and what condition the oven is in. On average between 2-3 hours.

What type of ovens do you clean?

We clean all types from single ovens to range ovens.

What is the process of cleaning my oven?

All racks from the oven are placed into a specially built van mounted heated dip tank along with back plates grill pans etc. We then manually clean the rest of the oven with our top of range non caustic cleaning materials. Once the racks, shelves etc have had long enough in the tank they are then rinsed and placed back in the oven, and in most cases make the oven look like new again.

What areas do you service for oven cleaning?

We cover most areas in the Devon area for oven cleaning including: Bideford, Barnstaple, South Molton, Tiverton, Exeter, Ilfracombe and Bude.
