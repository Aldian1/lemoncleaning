Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I want an end of tenancy clean?

The main advantage is to take the stress out of moving home. The advantage of using lemon cleaning is that we have the best cleaning equipment available on the market. We use a specially built heated dip tank to clean your oven professionally and the top of the range carpet cleaning machine to bring your carpets back to an excellent condition. We work closely with some of the major estate agents including Stags and Haarts and we always make sure they are happy with the property to enable you to get your deposit back. Lemon cleaning also has the capability to undertake the whole clean, including the oven, carpets and the deep clean which is required by most landlords so you wont have to worry about getting different contractors in to do specific works.

How long does an end of tenancy clean take?

Depending on the size of the property we would need a whole day to get it up to the highest standard possible to enable you to have a seamless transaction with the landlord or buying agent.

What areas do you service for end of tenancy cleaning?

We cover most areas in the Devon area for end of tenancy cleaning including: Bideford, Barnstaple, South Molton, Tiverton, Exeter and Bude.

What does an end of tenancy clean include?

- Clean inside all cupboards and drawers; as well as disposing of all items left in the kitchen such as old foodstuffs and carrier bags.
- Wash and polish all work surfaces.
- Clean sink and taps and remove limescale and polish where required.
- Wall tiles de-greased, cleaned and polished.
- Clean exterior and interior of all appliances.
- Bins cleaned inside and out.·            
- Cleaning oven to a professional standard·             
- Cleaning inside of windows.·             
- Cleaning skirting boards.

How can an end of tenancy clean save me money?

An end of tenancy clean carried out by a professional cleaning company like Lemon Cleaning will certainly get the property to the standard required for the return of your deposit. You have to remember an end of tenancy clean is purely a deep clean which is required by most landlords and letting agents. It is not a service to fix any damage that may have occurred in the property.
